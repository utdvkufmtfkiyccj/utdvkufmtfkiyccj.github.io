Hot Weather Zip Top
A quarter zip top designed for summertime running.

The Hot Weather Zip Top delivers outstanding summer running comfort via our unique new SAHARA ULTRA lightweight mesh fabric.

With its streamlined, four-panel construction, natural stretch and considered seam placement, this super-lightweight top favours functionality in warm weather above all else. Our SAHARA ULTRA mesh brings optimal breathability, an outstanding ability to transport moisture and dries in an instant.

Other key details include its quarter-length zip and dropped front collar for ventilation control, while longer cut sleeves offer further protection against harsh UV radiation.
Run easy in the scorching heat with the Hot Weather Zip Top.
