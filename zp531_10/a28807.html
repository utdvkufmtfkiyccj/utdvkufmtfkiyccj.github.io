コメント:Seven CD box set. Out Of This World: Live (1970 -1997) is a career-spanning box set, showcasing some of the biggest and most important live shows ELP have ever played. Encased in special, black, matte slipcase with hot foil finishes. Featured within the box set is a glossy photobook with intimate and unseen images of the band from legendary photographers Lynn Goldsmith and Neil Preston. Foreword written by Jerry Ewing editor of Prog magazine. Previously unreleased live show of Phoenix 1997 is included in set. High quality, fully-restored audio, mastered by ELP expert and renowned mastering engineer Andy Pearce.
