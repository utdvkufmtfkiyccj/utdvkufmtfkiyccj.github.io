説明: Instructions for Remote Control
Wireless Bluetooth remote control can control gopro shoot photos, video record and time-lapse photography.
The back of the remote control is magnetic and can be attached to the selfie stick, freeing your hands.
Green light is camera mode, red light is phone mode. Please turn off the remote control before shutting down.
For security use, we suggest to connect the remote with a strap when use it in a extreme sports like skiiing high speed riding.
Control range is about 40 meters in an open area.

Connect with GoPro Camera Mode:
1. Long press the on/off button of the remote control to start the machine and the indicator light flashes.
2. When the green light flashes --> go directly to the next step, when the red light flashes -->, long press the mode button until the green light flashes.
3. Turn on the Go Pro camera, swipe down, wipe left ? connect ? connect device ? go pro application.
4. Wait for the message that the camera has been successfully connected.
5. After the connection is successfully established, the green light flashes completely, press the photo button to take photos, and press the mode button to switch between the large mode of the camera (photography delay, video recording and photo recording mode).

Connection Phone Mode:
1. Long press the on / off key of the remote control to start the machine, and the indicator light flashes.
2. If the red light flashes -- > go directly to the next step. If the green light flashes -- >, long press the mode key until the red light flashes.
3. Turn on the mobile phone Bluetooth, search the "t-se" Bluetooth device name, and click pairing. The red light of the remote control flashes.
4. Turn on the mobile camera and press the Photo button briefly to take photos /
