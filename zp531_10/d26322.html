Long Sleeve Printed Tech T
A close-fitting, mid-weight long sleeved t-shirt.

Made from a mid-weight technical fabric the Printed Long Sleeve Tech-T offers breathability and enviable style; in limited seasonal SOAR prints.

The fit of the Printed Long Sleeve Tech-T has been revised, with a more tailored silhouette. Its Italian-made mesh fabric is soft against the skin and features elastane for added stretch, while overlocked seams ensure chafe-free movement over any distance. The new, closer fit also allows sweat to wick away more quickly, keeping you comfortable in a range of temperatures. The mid-weight technical fabric is treated with an antibacterial coating to control odour, while its long sleeves guard against the cool air on early morning starts and easy evening runs.

The Long Sleeve Tech-T is the running top to reach for when the weather, or mood, calls for something versatile and robust. Designed for maximum comfort and minimum fuss, it"s an essential piece of kit for cool everyday runs.
