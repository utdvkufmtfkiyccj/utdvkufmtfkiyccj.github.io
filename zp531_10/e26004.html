Run Shorts
Versatile, technical mid-length shorts for every day runs.

The Run Shorts bring light weight, comfort and high-performance to every day running. Boasting a relaxed fit and superior breathability, these mid-thigh shorts are designed to perform across a broad range of distances and intensities.

Constructed from a featherweight technical fabric, laser-cut ventilation holes at the sides boost breathability. While a bonded hem offers even greater comfort and a plush brief liner provides snugness and support. Notched hems on the outer leg bolster freedom of movement. A zipped pocket on the rear takes care of mid-run necessities.

The Run Shorts are an essential in any running wardrobe. Engineered for total comfort on the move, with highly technical fabric and innovative construction at their heart. They are a go-to pair of shorts when it comes to simply racking up the miles.

Pair any pair of Run Shorts with any Long Sleeve Tech T to complete a staple spring training system and save 15%
