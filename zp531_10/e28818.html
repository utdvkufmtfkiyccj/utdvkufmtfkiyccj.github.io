コメント:Operation: Mindcrime [4CD/DVD Box Set] Operation: Mindcrime was produced by Peter Collins and recorded, mixed, and mastered digitally on a Sony 24-track digital tape machine. This new version was remastered at Abbey Road Studios and brings together all the available recordings on 4 CDs. DVD contains promo videos and live performances. Features new sleeve notes by Alex Milas who spoke with Geoff Tate about the making of the album. Housed in a 10?x10? box, this is the final word on this exceptional album.
