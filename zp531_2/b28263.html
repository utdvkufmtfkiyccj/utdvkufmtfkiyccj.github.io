Glass roof sunshade for Tesla Model 3. This deluxe set includes one front roof, one rear roof, one front and rear roof, and one front and rear roof, plus two side triangle window mesh sunshades. Simply attach the included clip to your roof and snap in the sun shade for an ultra easy setup.Shade is easy to remove. Also, the rear roof shade is designed to ensure rear view and do not interfere with driving. Heat shielding sheets can be stacked over the sunshade.(Layer it on the outside of the sunshade) If you use it with the sunshade when the sun is particularly strong or when parked outdoors for a long time, it will reduce the temperature of your car and save your battery. It is very lightweight and can be folded into a compact size when not in use. Product Contents: 4 mesh shades (1 each for the front and back of the roof + 2 side triangle windows) / 2 heat shielding sheets (1 each for the front and rear of the roof) / Mounting clips The rear roof shade (those who have a large curve on one side) will not obstruct the rear view by attaching the curved side to the roof. Please layer the heat shielding sheet on the outside of the sunshade and the glossy side of the sunshade above.(It blocks heat by reflecting sunlight on a glossy surface)
