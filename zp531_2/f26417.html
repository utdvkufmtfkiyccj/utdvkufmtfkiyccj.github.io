This is a half size personal information protection sticker (blind sticker) It is a highly secure type that is not repositionable (removable). This sticker has a double layer structure and is composed of an opaque sheet (printed surface) above the "transparent film". When applied to postcards, etc., the postcards and "transparent film stickers", are strongly adhered to the surface, and the "impermeable sheet (printed surface) is removed from the weak adhered to the surface to read the hidden information. The impermeable sheet (printed surface) cannot be reattached once removed from the transparent film. Due to the structure, the sticker itself may be slightly curved (curl). If this happens, please use your hand to rebend the curve right before use. Works on any type of regular postcard paper (works with inkjet paper) It can also be used on a variety of other paper than postcards.
