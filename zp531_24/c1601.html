PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Brand new set of llor guitar parts humbucking ceramic magnet guitar pickups with 6 adjustable and 6 fixed pole pieces in black with a baseplate made of Nickel Silver which is used because of quality, durability, and sound and being that it is very hard and strong with durable threads for the height-adjustment screws. Many people can hear the difference between pickups with nickel-silver baseplates and those that use softer alloys like brass or pot l. The baseplate l has a direct effect on a pickup’s inductance. .
