説明: The Panda Wireless Express Router (PWRU01) is a versatile portable 2.4GHz 802.11n router.
You can use it as a WiFi Hotspot at hotel or on airplane to share an expensive wireless link with your laptop and smart devices.
It can turn any stereo system instantly into a music station (just connect them with a 3.55mm audio cable) in your home/car/boat.

Features

- Support IEEE 802.11 b/g/n standard (Max data rate: 150 Mbps)
- Support Wireless Router (default), Wireless Repeater and Client Modes
- Support iPhone/iPad/iTunes in Wireless Router Mode: Play music to your stereo system wirelessly
- Configure the Panda Wireless Express Router via its user"s friendly web interface
- Support standards-based security features: WPA-PSK and WPA2-PSK (TKIP+AES)

How it works

(1) In Wireless Router Mode (default): plug the Ethernet cable to the WAN port of the router from the DSL/Cable modem
to create your own wireless network
(2) In Wireless Repeater Mode: Switch the router into Wifi repeater via its web interface to extend the range of your network
or share a wireless link in hotel or on airplane with your local WiFi devices
(3) In Client Mode: connect your computer to the LAN port of the router using an Ethernet cable
(4) In Airplay mode: this mode works in Wireless Router Mode only
