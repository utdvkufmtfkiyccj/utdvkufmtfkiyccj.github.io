Overview:
The HDMI audio extractor extracts the digital audio signal from the HDMI input signal source and
converts it to 3.5mm stereo and optical output.Simple solution for your amplifier and HDMI equipment .

How to use HDMI Audio Extractor Converter function:
Connect display input to this audio extractor output port with HDMI Cable.
Connect your source output to this audio extractor input port with HDMI Cable.
Converter USB adapter or USB port of other devices to provide power for HDMI audio extractor.
Converter 3.5mm cable or optical cable connect to the A/V receivers .
